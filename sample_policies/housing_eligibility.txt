Eligibility for Council Housing

1. Applicants must be aged 18 or over.
2. Applicants must be a UK national or have indefinite leave to remain.
3. Priority is given to applicants who are homeless or at risk of homelessness.
4. Applicants with dependent children receive additional priority points.
5. Medical conditions that require specific housing adaptations are considered.
6. Applicants must have a local connection to the borough (residence, employment, or family ties).
7. Income thresholds apply: household income must not exceed £40,000 per year.
8. Applicants with rent arrears to a previous council tenancy may be deprioritised.
