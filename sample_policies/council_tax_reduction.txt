Council Tax Reduction Scheme

1. Council Tax Reduction is available to residents on a low income.
2. Applicants must be liable for council tax at their main residence.
3. Full reduction may be available to those receiving Income Support, income-based JSA, or Pension Credit.
4. Working-age applicants are assessed on household income and capital.
5. Capital over £6,000 is assumed to generate a weekly income of £1 per £250.
6. Non-dependant deductions apply when other adults live in the household.
7. A second adult rebate may apply where a single liable person shares with someone on a low income.
8. Applications must be made within one month of becoming liable.
