Universal Credit Entitlement

1. Universal Credit is available to working-age adults (18–66) who are on a low income or out of work.
2. Claimants must have less than £16,000 in savings.
3. The standard allowance depends on age and whether the claimant is single or in a couple.
4. Additional elements are available for children, housing costs, childcare, and disability.
5. Claimants must accept a Claimant Commitment outlining their responsibilities.
6. Work capability assessments may be required for those with health conditions.
7. Sanctions may be applied for failure to meet agreed-upon commitments.
8. Transitional protection applies for claimants migrating from legacy benefits.
